## When You Get Stuck

**No responses from Upwork?**
- Apply faster (<1 hour of posting)
- Target jobs with <5 proposals
- Improve proposal (see DEVELOPMENT.md templates)

**No response from outreach?**
- Follow up after 3 days
- Try different time (Tue-Thu 9-11am best)
- Shorten message
- Call instead of email

**Running out of time?**
- Focus on ONE track (usually Freelance fastest)
- Cut low-value activities
- Ask for help (VA, contractor)

**Losing motivation?**
- Remember: 4 weeks to prove viability
- Celebrate small wins (first bid, first response)
- Update CHANGELOG.md with progress
